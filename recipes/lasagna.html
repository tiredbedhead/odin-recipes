Lasagna Recipe

Description

Here is the recipe to my world famous lasagna.

Please enjoy!

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • sauce
  • seasoning
  • meat
  • cheese
  • fancy garnishing

Step-by-step cooking instructions

  1. cook pasta
  2. layer pasta with sauce and meat
  3. add seasoning everywhere
  4. put cheese in places
  5. cook for a certain amount of time
  6. when time is over - take out food
  7. add garnishing
  8. eat
